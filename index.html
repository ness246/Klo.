About

Modern puzzle gameplay meets classic Klotski challenge. Move the target block to the exit and solve each puzzle!

Klo. is a modern Klotski puzzle game developed with Pygame. The property sits at the meeting place of classic puzzle mechanics and innovative gameplay.

Known for its smooth drag-and-drop controls, the game offers everything needed for a full puzzle experience. It combines nostalgic 8-bit sounds with contemporary UI design, wrapped into original puzzle gameplay.

It's everything you need, ready to play on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
